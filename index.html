Pepe Jeans London is a globally recognized fashion brand founded in 1973 on Portobello Road, London. Starting as a small denim stall, it quickly grew into one of the most popular names in the world of casual wear.

The brand is best known for its premium denim, but its collections also include t-shirts, jackets, dresses, footwear, and accessories.
